Mars Rover

Move the Rover

Type "l" or "r" to rotate the robot left or right, and "b" or "f" to move the robot back or forward. Commands are case-insensitve, and can be chained together, such as "l,b,f".

Note: commands will execute with a one-second delay.

Transport the Rover

You can also just select a location where you want the rover to be, and which way you want it to be pointed. We'll transport the rover there. Like Star Trek, just FAR less exciting.
